Food, Medicine and Financial Assistance for our Teachers

As we face this Covid-19 crisis, Teachers are one of those people who has been greatly affected by this pandemic. We must not forget that they are the ones who built the foundations of our careers and of our society.

Due to the risk brought about by the coronavirus pandemic and the current state of Luzon pursuant to ECQ imposed by the national gov't, our teachers are struggling to make ends meet. As such, we, former students of Valenzuela Christian School (VCS), hope to reach out to you for the people we consider our second parents during this crisis. We have initiated this drive to aid them financially for their essential needs (such as but not limited to food, medicine). All the donations will be used to help our dear teachers. Any amount will be highly and sincerely appreciated.

You may also click the button below to let us know who you are. Rest assured that all of the data given are for transparency purposes only. It will not be shared to others.

For questions or concerns, please click on the Register button below

The organizers and our teachers would like to extend their gratitude for the warm support you are giving. Thank you very much!
